# TREET ACCEPTABLE USE FOR MERCHANTS
**Promotion and loyalty rules**

## 1. Purpose
Ensure that all promotions created within the App are legal, transparent and beneficial to Customers.

## 2. Mandatory rules for Merchants
The Merchant agrees that all its promotions:

### 2.1. Are clear and understandable
They must indicate precisely:
* Eligibility Requirements,
* number of points needed,
* conditions for obtaining the reward,
* validity date,
* other limitations if they exist (e.g., certain products not participating in the promotion).

### 2.2. Are truthful
* They cannot be misleading.
* They cannot promise rewards that are impossible to deliver.
* They cannot hide costs or restrictions.

### 2.3. Are executable in your business
The Merchant must be able to fulfill the offer for the entire period in which it is active.

### 2.4. Do not violate applicable legislation
Including:
* Consumer and user regulations
* Pricing regulations
* Trade promotion regulations
* Unfair competition law
* Health regulations (if applicable)
* Sectoral prohibitions (tobacco, alcohol for minors, etc.)

### 2.5. Don't be discriminatory
They must not be based on prohibited or unjustified criteria.

## 3. Express prohibitions
The Merchant may not:
* Create misleading, abusive, or unviable promotions.
* Post offensive, illegal, or inappropriate content.
* Create promotions aimed at manipulating internal statistics.
* Demand personal data from the Customer outside the App.
* Redirect the Customer to external systems to capture data.

## 4. Monitoring and measures
The App can:
* Automatically or manually review promotions.
* Temporarily suspend a promotion if it violates these rules.
* Terminate the Merchant account in the event of repeated or serious violations.
* Notify affected Customers in case of irregularities.

## 5. Liability
The Merchant assumes full responsibility for:
* The legality of your promotions
* The delivery of rewards
* Any claims arising from your offers
* Damages caused by breach or fraudulent behavior

The App will not be responsible for promotions created by Merchants.
