# Data Protection Policy
**Merchant (businesses)**

## 1. Who we are
Treet, www.treet-app.com ("we" or "the App") is the controller of the contact data of businesses ("Merchant") that sign up to create promotions. Contact: info@treet-app.com.

## 2. What data we process and how we obtain it
We collect only the data necessary to provide you with access to the Merchant panel and allow you to create promotions.

### A) Data provided by the Merchant (initial form)
* Business name
* Physical address
* Professional contact email
* Professional phone number

### B) Data collected by phone (business verification)
* VAT/CIF
* Name and surname of the contact person, owner, administrator or proxy
* Professional email
* Professional phone number
  *(Calls are not recorded. We only record the information necessary to verify the business).*

### C) Data generated by the use of the Merchant panel
* Account identifier
* Access history
* Date and time of creation or modification of promotions
* Technical data: IP, logs, device identifiers, security records

### D) Data publicly visible in the app (physical local businesses)
* Business name
* Address and location on the map
* Active promotions
  *(We never show personal data of the owner or contact person).*

## 3. Purposes of processing and legal bases
We process your data for:
* **Business verification**: pre-contractual measures and legitimate interest against fraud.
* **Registration management**: performance of the contract for access to the panel.
* **Publication**: performance of the contract to show the business and promotions in the app.
* **Metrics**: show aggregated performance metrics (do not include personal data of Customers).
* **Security**: legitimate interest for technical functioning and prevention of unauthorized access.
* **Legal obligations**: compliance with identification, taxation and accounting.

## 4. What we don't do
* We do not provide merchants with the identity or IDs of Customers, only aggregated metrics.
* We do not transfer data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Providers
We use Railway as our infrastructure provider (data processor) with formalized DPAs and security measures.

## 6. Conservation
| Type of data | Retention period | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Business data | Term of the contract + 5 years | Secure deletion |
| Contact data | Term + 5 years | Deletion |
| VAT/CIF | Term + 5 years | Deletion |
| Accounting data | 6 years (if applicable) | Mandatory by law |
| Technical logs | 3 months | Deletion |

## 7. Rights
You can request access, rectification, deletion, opposition, limitation and portability. You can delete your account from the App (Settings → Privacy → Delete account). You can also complain to the AEPD (www.aepd.es).

## 8. Security
We apply encryption in transit, segregation of environments, access control and backups.
