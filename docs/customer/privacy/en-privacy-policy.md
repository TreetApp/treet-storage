# Data Protection Policy
**Customer (end users)** 

## 1. Who we are
Treet, www.treet-app.com ("we" or "the App") is the controller of the personal data of end users ("Customer"). You can contact us at info@treet-app.com.

## 2. What we use your data for (purposes and legal basis)
We process your email (and the minimum technical data) to:
1. **Create and verify your account**: send verification code by email. 
   - **Legal basis**: performance of the contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).
2. **Allow the use of the App**: sign up for loyalty programs, redeem promotions, count uses. 
   - **Legal basis**: execution of the contract.
3. **Maintain the security of the service**: fraud prevention, technical records, access logs, IP. 
   - **Legal basis**: legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR).
4. **Show you nearby businesses**: on the map using your device's location only while you're using the app. 
   - **Legal basis**: performance of the contract/legitimate interest (we do not store your location; it is used at the time and does not leave the device).

**We do not send commercial communications** or transfer your data to third parties for marketing.

## 3. What data we process
* **Identification**: **email** (required).
* **Usage data**: loyalty programs you sign up for, redemptions, accumulation and use of promotions.
* **Minimum technical data**: IP, device identifiers, error/security logs.
* **Device location (optional and ephemeral)**: only to show distances and maps **without storing or recording** your location.

## 4. Who we share your data with
* We do not share your data with merchants. Businesses only see **aggregated statistics without identifying you**.
* **Technology providers**: we use **Railway** as a hosting/infrastructure provider (it acts as **a data processor**). We formalise an **order agreement (DPA)** and verify security measures and, where applicable, **standard contractual clauses** for international transfers.

## 5. Conservation
* **Active account**: we keep your data for as long as you maintain your account.
* **Account deleted by you**: immediate deletion of credentials and profile data. Strictly **necessary technical records** can be kept for 3 months for security and claims defense.
* **Promotion/redemption data**: during the term of the service and an additional 6 months for legal purposes only.

| Type of data | Retention period | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Email + compta | While active + 6 months | Total deletion after the deadline |
| Promotion history | While active | Anonymize when deleting account |
| Technical logs | 3 months | Deletion |
| Location | Not stored | - |


## 6. Your rights
You can request **access, rectification, deletion, opposition, limitation, portability** and **not be subject to automated decisions**. You can also **delete your account** from the App (**Settings → Privacy → Delete account**). If we do not attend to your request, you can complain to the **AEPD**.

## 7. Minors
The service is for **people over 14 years old**. We do not consciously remember data from minors. If we detect a minor's account, we will delete it.

## 8. Security
We apply **technical and organisational** measures proportional to the risk (encryption in transit, segregation of environments, access control, event logging and backups).

## 9. Changes
We will notify you in the App or by email if we make any relevant changes.
